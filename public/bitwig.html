Polarity Bitwig Tools

Bitwig is a DAW out of berlin. I did some free presets, devices and projects for everyone to download.

All listed tools have videos up on youtube. Would be nice when you subscribe my channel or vote my videos. Want to communicate or report broken links and errors? Visit our Bitwig Discord channel and say Hi! or drop me a message on Facebook.

Bitwig Devices

Bitwig devices chained together to build new powerful chain containers. Watch the complete Youtube Playlist.

  • EFFEKTWERK - Trigger FX with your midi keyboard! You know Effectrix, Turnado, Stutter Edit, Ableton Beat Repeater? It´s like that, for Bitwig and free.
    Download Effektwerk | Watch the video

  • KNISTER - It tries to simulate vinyl crackle noise with only bitwig devices.
    Download KNISTER | Watch the video

  • HALTMAL - It delays/loops the current audio forever. Press a midi note to reset the feedback loop and bypass the delay effect.
    Download HALTMAL | Watch the video

  • KRASSVERB - I patched some kind of weird reverb effect together. You can make pad sounds epic, wide, deep and dirty. Also it shows, Bitwig can do some nice reverb FX if you know how to chain. All default modules are pretty basic but chained together, they become very powerful.
    Download KRASSVERB | Watch the video

  • ULTIMATE KICK SNARE - 2 Chains for your daily Drum & Bass, Dubstep, EDM studio life. Created entirely in and with Bitwig modules. No Samples involved. Nice Macros for fast tuning.
    Download ULTIMATE KICK SNARE | Watch the video
